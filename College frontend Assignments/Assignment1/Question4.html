University Departments and Labs Information

Computer Science Department

The Computer Science department has multiple state-of-the-art labs to support students in software development, networking, and cybersecurity.

  • Software Development Lab: Equipped with high-performance computers and the latest development tools.
  • Networking Lab: Provides a simulated environment for network setup, testing, and security assessments.
  • Cybersecurity Lab: Specializes in hands-on learning for digital forensics and ethical hacking.

Electronics and Communication Engineering Department

The Electronics department is focused on practical training in circuit design, communication systems, and embedded systems development.

  • VLSI Lab: Focuses on digital and analog circuit design, and integrated circuits.
  • Embedded Systems Lab: Provides hands-on experience with microcontrollers and embedded programming.
  • Communication Systems Lab: Dedicated to wireless and optical communication experiments.

Mechanical Engineering Department

The Mechanical Engineering department offers a variety of labs for testing mechanical properties and working with advanced machinery.

  • Thermodynamics Lab: Offers experiments related to heat engines, refrigeration, and air conditioning systems.
  • CAD/CAM Lab: Equipped with computer-aided design and manufacturing tools for mechanical designs.
  • Strength of Materials Lab: Provides facilities to test the mechanical strength and durability of materials.

Biotechnology Department

The Biotechnology department is dedicated to research and experimentation in biological systems, genetic engineering, and microbiology.

  • Genetic Engineering Lab: Specializes in DNA extraction, cloning, and genetic analysis.
  • Microbiology Lab: Equipped for studying microorganisms, culture techniques, and testing biological materials.
  • Bioinformatics Lab: Provides computational tools and resources for biological data analysis.
